# 🐶 Puppy Pal

A cute pastel virtual-pet game! Adopt a **dog, cat, bunny, or mouse**, give it a name, and look after it. 💕

## ✨ Things you can do
- 🍖 Feed, 🎾 Play, 🛁 Bath, 😴 Sleep
- 🦮 Walk and 🏊 Swim mini-games
- 🎪 Teach tricks and 🏥 take it to the vet
- 🛒 Earn coins and shop for treats
- 🎀 Dress it up with bows, hats, capes and more
- 🐾 Make friends and 👋 meet them at the park
- 🌻 Grow a **cozy garden** — plant flower, bush & tree seeds, water them,
  watch butterflies & bees visit, and pick a bouquet for coins!
- 🎠 Tidy **sliding buttons** — tap the ‹ › arrows (or swipe) to flip slides

Your pet is **saved automatically** in your browser, and it **grows older** the more you play!

## ▶️ How to play (on your own device)
Just open **`index.html`** in any web browser — that's it, no installation needed.

## 🌐 Play online (tap-to-play link)
Once the website is turned on, you can play here:

### 👉 https://sunnyfunnykitty.github.io/puppy-pal/

**How to turn the website on (a grown-up does this once, ~2 minutes — all clicks!):**
1. Go to the repo: **github.com/sunnyfunnykitty/puppy-pal**
2. Click **⚙️ Settings** (top of the page).
3. In the left menu, click **Pages**.
4. Under **Build and deployment → Source**, choose **Deploy from a branch**.
5. For **Branch**, pick **`claude/puppy-pal-dev-llh99a`** and folder **`/ (root)`**, then **Save**.
   (That branch has all the newest features, and the live game updates by itself as we build!)
6. If GitHub says Pages needs a **public** repo (this happens on free accounts):
   go to **Settings → General → Danger Zone → Change visibility → Public**.
7. Wait about a minute, then open the link above. 🎉

💡 Tip: on an iPad, open the link in Safari, tap **Share → Add to Home Screen**,
and Puppy Pal becomes a tap-to-open app! 📱
